FAMILY HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY

Policy Number: FHP-2026-001
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Coverage Period: 12 months
Covered Members: Up to 6 family members

COVERED SERVICES:
1. Office Visits (CPT-99213, CPT-99214, CPT-99215)
   - Coverage: 90% after deductible
   - Copay: $20 per visit
   - Annual limit: 20 visits per family member

2. Diagnostic Tests
   - Complete Blood Count (CPT-85025): 85% coverage
   - Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CPT-80053): 85% coverage
   - Chest X-Ray (CPT-71020): 80% coverage
   - CT Scan Head/Brain (CPT-70450): 75% coverage
   - Annual limit: $5,000 per family

3. Emergency Services (CPT-99283)
   - Coverage: 85% after deductible
   - Copay: $100

4. Electrocardiogram (CPT-93000)
   - Coverage: 80%
   - Pre-authorization required for non-emergency

5. Surgical Procedures
   - Knee Arthroscopy (CPT-29881): 80% coverage
   - Pre-authorization required

DEDUCTIBLE: $750 per person, $2,000 family maximum
OUT-OF-POCKET MAXIMUM: $4,000 per person, $8,000 family maximum

NETWORK: HMO/PPO Hybrid Network
- In-network providers: Full coverage as listed
- Out-of-network providers: 60% of in-network coverage

PRE-AUTHORIZATION REQUIRED FOR:
- All imaging procedures (CT, MRI)
- Surgical procedures
- Hospital admissions
- Specialist referrals

ADDITIONAL BENEFITS:
- Preventive care: 100% coverage (annual checkups, vaccinations)
- Maternity care: 90% coverage
- Pediatric care: 95% coverage
- Prescription drugs: 75% coverage
- Vision care: $200 annual allowance per member
- Dental care: Basic coverage included
