BASIC HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY

Policy Number: BHP-2026-001
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Coverage Period: 12 months

COVERED SERVICES:
1. Office Visits (CPT-99213, CPT-99214, CPT-99215)
   - Coverage: 80% after deductible
   - Copay: $30 per visit
   - Annual limit: 12 visits

2. Diagnostic Tests
   - Complete Blood Count (CPT-85025): 70% coverage
   - Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CPT-80053): 70% coverage
   - Chest X-Ray (CPT-71020): 60% coverage
   - Annual limit: $2,000

3. Emergency Services (CPT-99283)
   - Coverage: 70% after deductible
   - Copay: $150

4. Electrocardiogram (CPT-93000)
   - Coverage: 60%
   - Requires pre-authorization

EXCLUDED SERVICES:
- CT Scans (CPT-70450) - Not covered
- Knee Arthroscopy (CPT-29881) - Not covered
- Elective surgeries - Not covered

DEDUCTIBLE: $1,000 per year
OUT-OF-POCKET MAXIMUM: $5,000 per year

NETWORK: PPO Network
- In-network providers: Full coverage as listed
- Out-of-network providers: 50% of in-network coverage

PRE-AUTHORIZATION REQUIRED FOR:
- All imaging procedures
- Surgical procedures
- Hospital admissions
